About Us

Oasis Canadian Schools Port Harcourt is the only accredited Canadian School in Nigeria, offering a unique blended curriculum that combines the best of Canadian and Nigerian education.

Our Mission

Our curriculum is designed to meet the graduation requirements of both the Nigerian and Canadian education systems. Eligible OCS students graduate with Nigerian certificates (WAEC and NECO) and a Canadian High School Diploma.

Unique Learning Program

OCS is a Canadian offshore school program of the Province of New Brunswick, Canada. The Canadian diploma is awarded by the New Brunswick Ministry of Education. The dual curricular training and certification provide OCS graduates a favorable competitive edge in their future academic and professional pursuits.

Extensive Global Opportunities

At Oasis Canadian Schools Port Harcourt, we empower our graduates to pursue their dreams in top Canadian and other universities. Our professional partners provide expert guidance that improves our students' success in obtaining scholarships, prestigious awards, admission to renowned institutions, and transitioning seamlessly to post-secondary education.

Awards and Recognition

Like other Canadian high schools, every year, OCS graduates receive the Governor General of Canada's Bronze Medal for Academic Excellence and Silver Birk Award for Leadership Excellence. Our graduates also receive the Canadian English Language Proficiency Assessment Report Card, an acceptable substitute to TOEFL and IELTS for admission to universities with no expiry date.

Proficient Professional Faculties

OCS teachers are the only Nigerian teachers with the training and skill to directly teach and assess students in classroom settings using the Canadian curriculum. Our faculty members stay updated on the latest developments in the Canadian education system, providing students with the most relevant and effective learning experience.

Excellent Learning System

OCS is a caring, safe, and orderly Canadian school with a state-of-the-art, purpose-built, serene learning community. The air-conditioned small classroom capacity (teacher:student of 1:4) and the individualized learning plan model make learning iterative, practical, and efficient.

Academic Programs

We offer a wide range of academic programs, including:

  • Core Courses: Sciences, Mathematics, Languages, Social Sciences, Technology and Computing, Business and Entrepreneurship, Health and Wellness
  • Elective Courses: Sciences and Technology, Social Sciences and Life Skills, Fine and Theater Arts, Skilled Trades and Technology Education, Business and Work Readiness, Career Pathways and Leadership Skills Development, Scholarship and Discovery
